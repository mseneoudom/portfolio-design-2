About Me

Hello! My name is Matthew, a front end developer based in the GTA with a passion for creating things! To me, coding is more than a hobby or even a job. It's a tool for creation, an outlet for creativity, and I'm excited to see what we can build together!

Prior to becoming a developer I completed two seperate programs within Accounting and Human Resources in college, as well as having a job closely related to my field of study. It was during that time I realized the aspects of a job that I loved. Problem solving, creating more efficient processes, being creative. This all lead to me discovering the wonders of coding and how it can be a powerful tool for creation. Fast forward a bit and here I am, an alumni of Juno College, Cohort 26!
